<p><strong>The Automotive Over-The-Air(OTA) Update Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Firmware over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update market encompasses two primary types: Firmware over-the-Air (FOTA) and Software over-the-Air (SOTA). FOTA involves updating the vehicle's firmware, which includes low-level software governing hardware functions, ensuring optimal performance and security. In contrast, SOTA focuses on higher-level software applications, including infotainment systems and navigation. Both types enhance vehicle functionality, enable rapid fixes, and improve user experience, while minimizing downtime and reducing the need for physical service visits, thus driving overall market growth.</p></p>
