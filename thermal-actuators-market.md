<p><p>The thermal actuators market encompasses several types, including squeeze type, diaphragm type, and piston type. Squeeze type actuators utilize heat to contract a material, enabling precise motion control. Diaphragm type actuators employ a flexible membrane that expands or contracts with temperature changes, providing rapid response in various applications. Piston type actuators feature a heated cylinder that drives a piston to create linear movement, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing automation and efficiency.</p></p>
