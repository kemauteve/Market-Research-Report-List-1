<p><ul><li>Time Charter</li><li>Voyage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry bulk shipping market is primarily divided into two main types: time charter and voyage market. In a time charter, a shipowner leases the vessel to a charterer for a specified period, allowing the charterer to control its operations while paying a fixed daily rate. Conversely, in the voyage market, the charterer pays for the shipment of goods on a per-voyage basis, covering costs like fuel and port fees. Each model offers distinct benefits and operational flexibility in transporting bulk commodities.</p></p>
