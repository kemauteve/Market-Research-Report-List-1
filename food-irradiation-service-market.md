<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Radiation</li><li>Gamma Radiation</li><li>X-Ray Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food irradiation service market comprises three main types: Electron Beam Radiation, Gamma Radiation, and X-Ray Radiation. Electron Beam Radiation utilizes high-energy electrons to quickly treat food, effectively eliminating pathogens and extending shelf life. Gamma Radiation employs gamma rays from radioactive isotopes, providing deep penetration suitable for bulk food sterilization. X-Ray Radiation combines the benefits of both electron and gamma technologies, offering precise control and high penetration levels, making it ideal for a range of food products while ensuring safety and quality.</p></p>
