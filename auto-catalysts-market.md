<p><ul><li>Two Way Catalyst</li><li>Three Way Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalyst market is primarily divided into two types: two-way catalysts and three-way catalysts. Two-way catalysts are designed to reduce carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, commonly used in diesel engines. In contrast, three-way catalysts simultaneously convert carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides, making them essential for gasoline engines to meet stringent emission regulations. Both catalyst types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and ensuring compliance with environmental standards, driving their demand in the global automotive market.</p></p>
