<p><strong>The Fire Alarm Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addressable Systems</li><li>Conventional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire alarm systems are primarily categorized into addressable and conventional types. Addressable systems allow for the individual identification of each detection device, enabling precise monitoring and faster response times. They are suitable for larger, complex environments. In contrast, conventional systems group devices into zones, triggering alarms based on collective signals, making them simpler and generally less expensive. While effective for smaller areas, they offer less specificity in pinpointing the exact source of a fire.</p></p>
