<p><p>Mini-split air conditioners, also known as ductless systems, provide efficient cooling solutions for residential and commercial spaces without the need for ductwork. These systems consist of an outdoor compressor unit and one or more indoor units, allowing for zoned temperature control and improved energy efficiency.
