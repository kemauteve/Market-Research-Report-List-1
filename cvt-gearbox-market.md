<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CVT gearbox market comprises various types, including belt-chain drive CVTs and planetary CVTs. Belt-chain drive CVTs utilize a seamless belt or chain to adjust the gear ratio, providing smooth and efficient power transfer, often seen in scooters and eco-friendly vehicles. Planetary CVTs, on the other hand, employ a set of gears arranged in a planetary configuration, offering compact design and high torque efficiency, making them suitable for a wide range of automotive applications, including hybrids and advanced transmissions.</p></p>
