<p><p>The disposable latex gloves market is categorized into two main types: powdered gloves and no powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a light dusting of cornstarch, which facilitates easy wearing and removal, making them popular in various settings. However, they can pose a risk of allergic reactions and contamination. No powdered gloves are free from any powder, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and making them preferable in medical and food-related applications, where hygiene is paramount.</p></p>
