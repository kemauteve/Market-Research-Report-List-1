<p><p>The Ultra Long Distance Optical Transmission Subsystem Equipment market is categorized into two main types: No SBS Suppression and SBS Suppression. </p><p>No SBS Suppression systems operate without specific mechanisms to mitigate Stimulated Brillouin Scattering (SBS), a phenomenon that can degrade signal quality over long distances. These systems are simpler and more cost-effective but may have limitations in range and signal integrity. </p><p>SBS Suppression systems incorporate technologies to minimize SBS effects, enabling higher quality signals over extended distances, thus supporting more demanding applications in communications and data transfer.</p></p>
