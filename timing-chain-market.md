<p><ul><li>Roller Chain</li><li>Silent Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The timing chain market is primarily divided into two types: roller chains and silent chains. Roller chains are commonly used for their simplicity and durability, where individual links interlock to transmit power effectively. They are widely employed in various machinery and automotive applications. Silent chains, on the other hand, feature a unique tooth and link design that minimizes noise and vibration, making them ideal for high-speed and precision applications. Both types cater to specific needs in industrial and automotive sectors, emphasizing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
