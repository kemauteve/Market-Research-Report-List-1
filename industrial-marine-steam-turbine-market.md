<p><ul><li>Condensing Steam Turbine</li><li>Non-Condensing Steam Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial and Marine Steam Turbine market comprises two primary types: condensing and non-condensing steam turbines. Condensing steam turbines are designed to maximize efficiency by converting exhaust steam into water, allowing for the recovery of heat and optimal energy use. In contrast, non-condensing steam turbines exhaust steam directly into the atmosphere, often used in applications where heat recovery is less critical. Both types serve various industries, including power generation and marine propulsion, catering to specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
