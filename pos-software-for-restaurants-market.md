<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POS software for restaurants is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises systems. Cloud-based POS solutions operate on remote servers, allowing for easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers flexibility, automatic updates, and scalability. In contrast, on-premises POS systems are installed locally on the restaurant's hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different operational needs and budget considerations for restaurants.</p></p>
