<p><strong>The Glow Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glow Tube market consists primarily of two types: Ferroresonant and Tap Switching. Ferroresonant glow tubes utilize a magnetic core to maintain steady output voltage, making them suitable for applications requiring stable performance. In contrast, Tap Switching glow tubes adjust voltage levels through discrete taps on a transformer, allowing for flexibility in operation and energy efficiency. Both types are essential in various industrial and residential applications, providing reliable voltage regulation while supporting different operational needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
