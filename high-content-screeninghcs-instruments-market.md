<p><p>High Content Screening (HCS) instruments encompass two main market types: cell imaging and analysis systems, and flow cytometers. Cell imaging and analysis systems capture detailed images of cells to evaluate morphological changes and intracellular events, enabling researchers to conduct thorough assays. Flow cytometers, on the other hand, analyze the physical and chemical characteristics of particles in a fluid as they pass through a laser beam, allowing for rapid cell population analysis and sorting. Both technologies enhance drug discovery and biological research.</p></p>
