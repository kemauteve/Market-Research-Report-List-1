<p><strong>The Automatic Wafer Aligner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Aligner</li><li>Notch Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Wafer Aligner market includes two primary types: Flat Aligners and Notch Aligners. Flat Aligners are designed to achieve high precision alignment of wafers without specific reference features, making them suitable for various wafer types and sizes. In contrast, Notch Aligners utilize built-in notches or alignment features on the wafer, allowing for rapid and accurate positioning, especially in applications with predefined geometries. Both aligners are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing for improving yield and efficiency in wafer processing.</p></p>
