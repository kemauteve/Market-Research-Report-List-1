<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) discrete devices market includes SiC MOSFETs, SiC diodes, and SiC modules, each serving distinct applications. SiC MOSFETs are used for high-efficiency power management due to their fast switching speeds and high thermal conductivity. SiC diodes provide enhanced reliability in high-voltage applications, offering lower reverse recovery losses. SiC modules integrate multiple components, optimizing space and improving system performance in power electronics. Together, these devices support advancements in renewable energy, electric vehicles, and industrial automation.</p></p>
