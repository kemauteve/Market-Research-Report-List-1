<p><strong>The Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal detector market features three main types: Pulse Induction (PI), Very Low Frequency (VLF), and Beat-frequency Oscillation (BFO). PI detectors excel in depth detection and are ideal for challenging environments, making them suitable for underwater and treasure hunting. VLF machines operate at lower frequencies, providing high sensitivity to small metal objects, popular among hobbyists. BFO detectors use two oscillators, offering simplicity and affordability, but with lower sensitivity, making them suitable for beginners or basic applications.</p></p>
