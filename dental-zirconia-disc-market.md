<p><p>The dental zirconia disc market is categorized into three types based on transparency levels: high transparency, medium transparency, and low transparency. High transparency zirconia is ideal for esthetic restorations, closely resembling natural teeth, making it suitable for anterior applications. Medium transparency offers a balance between strength and aesthetics, often used for both anterior and posterior restorations. Low transparency zirconia is predominantly used for its durability in posterior applications, where esthetics are less critical but strength is essential.</p></p>
