<p><p>The Automatic Bottled Labeling Machine market includes three main types: Self-adhesive/Pressure-sensitive labelers, which use adhesive labels that stick directly to the bottle; Shrink sleeve labelers, where a shrinkable plastic sleeve wraps around the bottle and is shrunk using heat; and Glue-based labelers, which apply a layer of adhesive to attach paper or plastic labels. Each type offers unique advantages in efficiency, durability, and aesthetic appeal, catering to various packaging needs across industries.</p></p>
