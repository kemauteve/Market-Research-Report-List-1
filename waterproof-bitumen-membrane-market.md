<p><p>The waterproof bitumen membrane market comprises two primary types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene) membranes. SBS membranes are flexible and offer excellent elasticity and tensile strength, making them ideal for environments with temperature fluctuations. In contrast, APP membranes are known for their superior UV resistance and high-temperature stability, making them suitable for exposed applications. Both types are widely used in construction for roofing and waterproofing, providing vital protection against moisture infiltration.</p></p>
