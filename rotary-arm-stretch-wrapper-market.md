Semiautomatic Stretch Wrappers automate the wrapping process but still require operator involvement, making them suitable for medium-scale production. Automatic Stretch Wrappers provide fully automated wrapping, enhancing efficiency and speed for high-volume operations. Each type caters to different production needs and volume requirements in packaging.</p></p>
