<p><strong>The Foodborne Pathogen Detection Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foodborne pathogen detection kit market primarily features two main types: PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). PCR is a molecular technique that amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling rapid and precise identification of pathogens. In contrast, ELISA is an immunological method that uses antibodies to detect antigens associated with pathogens, providing results based on colorimetric changes. Both techniques are crucial for ensuring food safety and preventing outbreaks caused by contaminated food products.</p></p>
