<p><p>Chemical protective suits are categorized into six types based on their level of protection and intended use. Type-1 suits offer full encapsulation against specific chemicals, while Type-2 suits provide a lower level of protection with limited air permeability. Type-3 suits are designed for liquid chemical protection with a barrier to liquid penetration. Type-4 suits focus on splash protection, whereas Type-5 and Type-6 suits are intended for particulates and light liquid splashes, respectively, offering varying degrees of protection and comfort for different applications.</p></p>
