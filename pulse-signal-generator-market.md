<p><p>The pulse signal generator market is divided into two main types: digital and analog signal generators. Digital signal generators produce precise, discrete signals for modern electronic applications, often featuring advanced functionalities like waveform customization and digital modulation. In contrast, analog signal generators deliver continuous waveforms, commonly used in testing analog devices. Each type serves distinct industry needs, with digital generators favored in digital electronics, telecommunications, and data transmission, while analog generators find applications in audio equipment and traditional testing environments.</p></p>
