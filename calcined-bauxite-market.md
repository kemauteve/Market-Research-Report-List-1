<p><p>The calcined bauxite market encompasses two primary forms: aggregate and powder. Aggregate bauxite is typically large granules used in applications like asphalt, road construction, and as a friction surface in pavements due to its high durability and resistance. In contrast, calcined bauxite powder is finely processed for use in the production of aluminum, refractories, and specialty ceramics. Both forms are essential in various industries, capitalizing on their unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
