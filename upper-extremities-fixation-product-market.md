<p><p>The Upper Extremities Fixation Products market comprises several key types aimed at stabilizing fractures and injuries. Internal fixation groups include plates, screws, nails, and other devices that are surgically implanted to hold bones together during healing. Plates provide surface support, screws offer point stabilization, and nails are inserted within the bone. External fixation involves devices applied outside the body, providing support while allowing for some mobility. Together, these products enhance recovery and restore functionality for upper limb injuries.</p></p>
