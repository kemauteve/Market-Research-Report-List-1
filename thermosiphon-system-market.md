<p><strong>The Thermosiphon system Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulation System</li><li>Direct System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosiphon system market can be categorized into circulation systems and direct systems. Circulation systems utilize a pump to enhance fluid movement, ensuring efficient heat transfer and consistent temperature regulation. In contrast, direct systems rely on natural convection, allowing heated fluid to rise and cool fluid to descend without mechanical assistance. While circulation systems offer greater control and can be integrated with renewable energy sources, direct systems are simpler and cost-effective, making them suitable for residential and small-scale applications.</p></p>
