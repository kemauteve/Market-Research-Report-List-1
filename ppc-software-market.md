<p><p>PPC software markets are categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based PPC software is hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy access from any device with an internet connection. This model typically involves a subscription fee and allows for automatic updates. On-premises PPC software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing control over data and customization options, but often requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
